Powerful Features for Seamless Collaboration

CollabNotes brings everything together in one place so your team can collaborate without missing a beat.

Real-Time Collaboration

See edits as they happen. Multiple people can work on the same note simultaneously.

Document History

Track changes and revert to any previous version with our comprehensive version history.
